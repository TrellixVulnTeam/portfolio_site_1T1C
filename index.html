Self-Hosted Searx Instance

Searx is a "hackable" search aggregator that allows users to search across multiple internet search engines at once. Public instances of searx tend to be flagged by the bot filters of commonly aggregated search engines due to volume of use. To avoid this issue, I spun up a personal instance of searx on a Digital Ocean VPC.
